About

I want to learn everything you can offer.  I moved to Malmö for 4 years ago. I have a full-time job at the IKEA Malmö store. During my studies I'm on leave, but I still work every other weekend. My main department is Livingroom where sofas, bookshelf's/tv benches and workspace are included. I have broad assignment which includes being responsible over bookshelf's and tv-benches. I also have been responsible for the forecast on the range at the Livingroom department.
In addition to this the focus is helping and serving customers to meet their expectations, keeping our department in shape as new by making it inspiring and showing the right furniture's.  I also have competence at home textile/ bed textiles and have helped logistic picking orders for the customers and unpacking.  I always meet the customer and my colleagues with a smile on my face and I feel very appreciated at my work. My ambition is to always leave a smile on their faces too.  I would love to keep develop and learn new things, broadening my understanding and competence within UX-Design.
